Recovery Point Objective (RPO) Example

Understanding RPO in a Financial Company Scenario

Scenario: Recovery Point Objective (RPO)

Imagine a financial company processing online transactions with an RPO of 15 minutes.

Normal Operations

The company constantly processes transactions and updates its database.

Data Backup Frequency

Data backups or replication occur every 15 minutes to meet the RPO requirements.

Incident Occurs

A hardware failure causes the primary database to become unavailable.

Recovery Process Initiates

The company restores data from the most recent backup taken before the incident.

Data Loss Mitigation

The recovery process ensures that the company loses, at most, the transactions from the last 15 minutes.
